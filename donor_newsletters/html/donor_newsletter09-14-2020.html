United We Help

This year, as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic, Activate Good is continuing its tradition of honoring those lost on September 11, 2001 with an annual day of service in a new format. We hope you'll join us in a restorative month of service, learning about local causes, and committing to improving our community - together. We are calling September's month-long initiative: United We Help!

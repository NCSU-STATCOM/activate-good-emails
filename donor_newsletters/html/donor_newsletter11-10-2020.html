Activate Good to host at-home Family Volunteer Day in November

 

﻿Behind the Scenes at Activate Good﻿

Teens and their parents (and my mom, Vickie!) helped us prep for Family Volunteer Day. We assembled a few of the kits that will be available, including Hygiene Baggies for People Experiencing Homelessness, Decorated Holiday Message for Seniors, Good in a Flash Student Study Kits, Wreaths for a Family’s New Home, Adoptable Pet Care Packs, No Place Like Home String Art Kits! We hope to see you on Family Volunteer Day - Saturday, November 21, 2020.

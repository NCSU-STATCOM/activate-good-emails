IT'S HAPPENING

We are SO EXCITED to finally announce our first major step towards realizing the Good Hub dream:

The Good Hub POP UP, coming this March and April!

Thanks to the generosity of Colliers International Raleigh Durham, we will be able to pilot-test a few of the core features of the future Good Hub this spring in a temporary location -- 702 Oberlin Road in Raleigh -- and we want YOU to join us! We're in the process of developing our Good Hub POP UP schedule now, but keep an eye on our Good Hub site for more information in the coming days to learn how you can visit for a tour, participate in (or collaborate on!) a special event in the space, or drop in with your friends and family.

Special thanks to Oak City Productions for creating this announcement video for us and helping us make this announcement in style!

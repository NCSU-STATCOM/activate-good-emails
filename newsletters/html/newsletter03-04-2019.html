Teens Change the World!

2019 Summer Program

June 17-21 or July 15-19

The Teens Change the World! Summer Program is a week-long service and leadership experience that will give teens the opportunity to volunteer with different nonprofits (15 service hours in one week!), learn about a variety of local causes, learn from social impact and business experts, and even pitch their ideas for social impact for the chance to win a small grant to execute their idea!

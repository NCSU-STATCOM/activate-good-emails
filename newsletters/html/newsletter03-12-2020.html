Activate Good News | March 2020

Response to COVID-19

Activate Good takes the spread of COVID-19 very seriously. We are also seeing the deep impact the loss of volunteer support is having on the critical work of the nonprofit partners we serve in our community. 

Learn about the measures we're taking to protect staff and volunteers, our continuing mission to serve our nonprofit partners who need volunteers now more than ever, and how we're handling activities in our newly launched Good Hub POP UP here.


Activate Good will continue its mission of supporting local nonprofits and connecting volunteers in good health to fulfill their needs to the extent that is possible and safe at this time. We are also compiling a list of ways that volunteers can continue providing critical support to area nonprofits through remote volunteer opportunities and hope you will consider helping in these ways if you are able. This ongoing list of remote volunteer needs can be seen here.

Thank you all for the amazing support you give Activate Good and causes in our community every day. Please stay tuned for additional ways we can work to support each other during this time while staying safe as a community.

 

March Volunteer

Opportunity Highlights


Volunteer support for Triangle area causes is dropping rapidly in response to the community's concerns over the spread of Coronavirus. Our community's most vulnerable populations will be greatly impacted by this dramatic decrease in support. This month, Activate Good is highlighting many of the ways you can help the community, including ways to help remotely from your home or workspace.

To continue regular volunteering


If you are able, please consider supporting local nonprofits by volunteering in person. Please note each nonprofit partner may have a different policy regarding their activities due to COVID-19. Please be sure to inquire about your nonprofit host's policy in the event you choose to sign up to volunteer. Browse regular volunteer needs here.

Remote volunteering


If you are in good health but your work or circumstances have required you to cancel or opt out of other public activities, other ways you can continue to support area causes in this time of need are on this list.

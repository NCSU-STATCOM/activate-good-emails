HOW YOUR DONATIONS CREATE A RIPPLE OF IMPACT

Your support is an investment in the people, places, and future of the Triangle. Your support will enable us to take on projects just like...

Operation Access: Our initiative completed mid-April in partnership with Wake County Public School Systems to mobilize volunteers to prep 10,000+ hotspots so kids without internet access could learn from home alongside their peers.

Operation Hand Up: Our current initiative to mobilize volunteers to help dozens of neighbors experiencing homelessness apply for stimulus and unemployment support.

Teens Change the World: Our initiative that mobilizes Triangle area teens in service and provides community leadership training so they can become the next generation of local changemakers!

...And SO much more in service to hundreds of needs and causes around the Triangle! Thank you for any amount you can give during these challenging times. Every penny, every dollar helps immensely.

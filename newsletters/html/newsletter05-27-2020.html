Sharing top insights from our Community Partner Coronavirus Challenges Report

﻿

﻿In April 2020, the Activate Good team conducted interviews with 45 local nonprofits to take the pulse on our community partner network. The purpose of these interviews was to assess how Activate Good can be impactful and effective in our work to mobilize volunteers to support the community, both in the coming months and long-term. These interviews allowed us to understand our Community Partners’ needs and explore the possibilities of how volunteers can be a part of helping the Triangle become stronger during the Coronavirus pandemic and into the future.

Learn more about Activate Good's plans to meet community needs and our top insights from our interviews in our new Community Partner Coronavirus Challenges report.

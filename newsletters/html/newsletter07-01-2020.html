Thank You!

SmART Summer Supply Drive


Because of your generous support, the SmART Summer Supply Drive we conducted in partnership with Art Intimacy will provide art supplies to an estimated 250 youth served by our community partners, including Haven House Services, the Salvation Army of Wake County, and Raleigh Rescue Mission.

Thank you to all of our generous donors for this initiative, whether you dropped off items to our donation box or purchased something from our Amazon Wish List! Please note that Amazon does not automatically share your names with us, so we only have the names of those who provided them to us.

URGENT CALL FOR VOLUNTEERS!

Volunteer and be a part of an urgent mission to ensure kids in Wake County have the technology they need to participate in learning alongside their peers this Fall.

For Operation Access Part 2, volunteers will help hand out 40,000+ laptops to students and families across 5 different sites in Wake County to ensure all students have reliable technology access and can participate in remote learning.

Volunteer shifts are 3-4 hours each and start August 17 and last until available laptops have been distributed.

What are these Signs for Good?

Signs for Good are a bright and easy way to show your support for kindness, community, volunteerism, unity and goodwill. These yard signs have a simple message:

Do Good. Be Kind. Change the World.

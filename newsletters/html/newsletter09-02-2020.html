Monthly Giving Program - amplify your impact!

Did you know? If we can recruit 200 new Monthly givers, we'll earn a challenge grant of $25,000! Help us meet our goal - join by completing this form!

Invest in our impacts with accessible monthly donations of $5, $10, $20 (or other amounts!) — while seeing your investments at work and being part of a generous community of like-minded do-gooders in our area.

Fun fact: If you sign up as a monthly giver and choose for your membership to be part of our Youth Givers Program, we’ll address our thank you note and future correspondence to your child as a fun way of including them in the giving process!


We believe we all have a responsibility to work towards a better world. That’s why Activate Good improves our community through people-powered projects for good.

Save the Date! November 21 is Family Volunteer Day, the "Take-Home Service Project Kit" Edition!

This year, Activate Good's annual Family Volunteer Day will be on Saturday, November 21, but in a NEW socially-distanced format - with pick-up-and-take-home service project kits and other goodies for families with kids of all ages to enjoy! These Take-Home Service Project kits will be available on a pay-what-you-can donation amount basis to help Activate Good cover the costs of materials and hosting the socially-distanced kit pick-up event.

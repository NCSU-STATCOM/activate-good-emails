Hunger & Homelessness Awareness Week

Hunger and Homelessness Awareness Week (November 15-22) is an annual program where people come together across the country to draw attention to and to address the problems of hunger and homelessness.

This year, hunger and homelessness are even more apparent in the Triangle due to the pandemic and its impacts felt in communities globally. Join Activate Good to volunteer between November 15 and 22 at one of several volunteer projects tackling hunger and homelessness in our community.

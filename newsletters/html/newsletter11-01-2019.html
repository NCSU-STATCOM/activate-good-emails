Join Activate Good on Saturday, November 23 for Family Volunteer Day, a Day of Service that celebrates the power of families who volunteer together while supporting their local communities. Families can sign up for one of two possible shifts at the Southeast Raleigh YMCA to participate in kid-friendly service projects, enjoy complimentary refreshments, and have fun doing good together! Special thanks to WakeMed, this year's Family Volunteer Day top sponsor!

Hunger & Homelessness

Awareness Week (Nov 15-22)

This year, hunger and homelessness are even more apparent in the Triangle due to the pandemic and its impacts felt in communities globally.

Join Activate Good to volunteer between November 15 and 22 at one of several highlighted volunteer projects tackling hunger and homelessness in our community, including but not limited to: 

  • Conducting a supply drive and assembling Family Essentials Kits
  • Adopting a volunteer shift at the Food Bank in Raleigh or Durham
  • Bagging food items for Thanksgiving with TABLE in Carrboro

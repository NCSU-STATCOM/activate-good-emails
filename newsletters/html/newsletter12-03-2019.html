Support our work to help build a community

of compassion in action

What if we could live in a community in which every person truly saw the power they had to create positive change--and was taking action to make the world a better place?

This is the vision that drives Activate Good. We don't promote volunteering just because it's a "good thing to do". We inspire, encourage, and connect locals to service because everyone taking action makes the whole community better for all.

This #GivingTuesday, contribute to our campaign to help us serve MORE great causes and mobilize more locals to volunteer in 2020!

Your donations help us...

Plan and host Days of Service that mobilize thousands of volunteers to support area causes.

Connect thousands of volunteers to over 500 causes in the Triangle each year, at NO cost to these organizations.

Host our Teens Change the World! Summer Program, during which we engage local teens in service throughout the week and help them develop a social impact service project, which we fund and provide mentorship to execute afterward.

Meet our 2020-2021 fundraising goals to upfit and launch The Good Hub, a first-of-its-kind in the nation community center with a special mission of inspiring people to Do Good!

And so much more!

Thank you for your friendship and support this year.

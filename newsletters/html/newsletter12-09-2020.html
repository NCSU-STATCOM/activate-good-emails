As part of our Activate MORE Good year-end campaign, we’re celebrating the good things we accomplished together in 2020 with 21 stories of good. 

Visit www.activategood.org/activatemoregood throughout December to contribute to our work and see all 21 stories as we unveil them! Check out two stories - one from Activate Good volunteer project leader Meka, and another from Kevin with WCPSS, below.

Introducing: Donate Stocks

Activate Good is excited to announce an exciting new giving opportunity - our capability to accept stock donations!

While tax laws are constantly changing, we felt that this was an imperative tool that we wanted to place in our donors' toolbox, as this may provide you specific tax benefits all while helping to fuel People-Powered Projects in the Triangle. (Be sure to consult your tax professional before engaging in this transaction).

In order to help continue our mission, please contact our Development Coordinator, Lee Posey Pike directly for specific instructions on how to participate in this program or download this form to get started.

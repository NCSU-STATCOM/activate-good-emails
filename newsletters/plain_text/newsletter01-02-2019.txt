Activate Good News | January 2019

Happy New Year!

[2]

The new year is the perfect time to set new goals. If one of your goals is
to give back to the community, browse the highlighted volunteer
opportunities below or visit [3]activategood.org to find an opportunity
that fits you!

Highlights

USE YOUR SKILLS

SKILLS-BASED OPPORTUNITIES

[4]Grant Writer and Researcher

Ongoing Commitment

International Focus is in need of someone to help research and write grants
on a regular basis.

[5]Basketball and Tennis Instructors

3 month Commitment

SportsWorks is in need of basketball and tennis coaches to work with
at-risk youth in the Spring.

[6]Use your Legal Knowledge for Good

Ongoing Commitment

SOAR Outreach is in need of an attorney to provide education on the
juvenile justice system.

PLANT YOUR ROOTS

ONGOING OPPORTUNITIES

[7]Miracle League of the Triangle

Ongoing Commitment!

Volunteers are needed to take on a variety of tasks related to sports
leagues for folks with special needs.

[8]NC Therapeutic Riding Center

Ongoing Commitment

Take on a variety of tasks to help folks with disabilities learn to ride
horses as therapy. Volunteers with and without horse experience accepted.

[9]Free Food Fridays at Salvation Army

Ongoing Commitment

Salvation Army of Durham needs volunteers every Friday morning to help
setup and serve food to those in need.

QUICK IMPACTS

ONE-TIME OPPORTUNITIES

[10]First Friday Fun at ArtSpace

Friday, January 4

Help ArtSpace provide food and drinks to the variety of folks to pass
through during the the First Friday ArtWalk!

[11]Distribute firewood with Warmth for Wake

Saturday, January 12 + 26

Cut, split, and deliver firewood to low income residents of Wake County
with Wake County Human Services.

[12]Deliver Grocery Bags to Seniors in Durham

Thursday, January 17

Help Inter-Faith Food Shuttle deliver bags filled with nutritious meals to
senior citizens living in low-income housing authorities.

[13]Activate Good believes when community members step up and give their
time and talents through service, real problems get solved. We're a
nonprofit activating volunteers to help charitable causes in our local
community. We connect individuals, groups, and companies to fulfill
volunteer needs with over 460 nonprofits around the Triangle!

What are these Signs for Good?

[7]Signs for Good are a bright and easy way to show your support for
kindness, community, volunteerism, unity and goodwill. These yard signs
have a simple message:

Do Good. Be Kind. Change the World.

Proceeds support Activate Good’s efforts to inspire helping others and
kindness in action through service! [8]Learn more.

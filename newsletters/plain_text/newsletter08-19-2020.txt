VOLUNTEERS STILL NEEDED for Weeks 2 and 3! (August 24 - September 4)

Volunteer (ages 18+ only) and be a part of an urgent mission to ensure kids
in Wake County have the technology they need to participate in learning
alongside their peers this Fall.

For [20]Operation Access Part 2, volunteers will help hand out 40,000+
laptops to students and families across 5 different sites in Wake County to
ensure all students have reliable technology access and can participate in
remote learning. Volunteer shifts are 3-4 hours each and start August 17
and last until available laptops have been distributed.﻿

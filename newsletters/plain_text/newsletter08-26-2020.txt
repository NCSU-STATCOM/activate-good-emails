VOLUNTEERS STILL NEEDED!

Be a part of an urgent mission to ensure kids in Wake County have the
technology they need to participate in learning alongside their peers.

An estimated 10,000 laptops have been distributed so far - and hot spot
distributions will begin this week. Volunteers are needed!

[22]See details / sign up HERE

For [23]Operation Access Part 2, volunteers will help WCPSS distribute
laptops and hot spots to students and families across 5 different sites in
Wake County. Volunteer shifts are 3-4 hours each and last until available
laptops have been distributed. We still need help [24]THIS WEEK AND NEXT!

Saturday, November 21 is [5]Family Volunteer Day:

﻿the "Take-Home Service Project Kit" Edition!

Presented by [6]Lenovo

[7]

This year, Activate Good's annual Family Volunteer Day will be on Saturday,
November 21, but in a NEW socially-distanced format -
with pick-up-and-take-home service project kits and other goodies for
families with kids of all ages to enjoy! These Take-Home Service Project
kits will be available on a pay-what-you-can donation amount basis to help
Activate Good cover the costs of materials and hosting the
socially-distanced kit pick-up event.
